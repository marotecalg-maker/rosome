Privacy Policy

Rosome — anime discovery & tracker

Last updated: 21 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Rosome ("the app", "we") handles information. In short: Rosome does not require an account and does not collect, store, or share your personal information. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

The short version
  • No sign-up, no login, no personal account.
  • Your watchlist, ratings, progress, and settings are stored only on your device.
  • No analytics, no advertising, no tracking, no data selling.
  • We never ask for your name, email, location, contacts, camera, or microphone.

1. Information we do not collect

Rosome does not collect personally identifiable information. We do not have a server that stores your data, and there is no account system. We cannot see your library, your ratings, or how you use the app.

2. Data stored on your device

The app saves the following locally on your device only, so it works offline and remembers your choices:

  • Your tracked anime and their status (watching, completed, planned, etc.).
  • Episode progress, personal ratings, and favourites.
  • Your preferences (theme and language).

This information never leaves your device. If you delete the app (or clear its data), this information is permanently removed.

3. Third-party services

To show anime information, the app connects to the following services. No personal identifiers are attached to these requests.

  • Jikan API (MyAnimeList data) — When you browse or search, the app requests anime titles, descriptions, scores, and cover images from jikan.moe. Only the anime you look up (e.g. a search term or an anime ID) is sent — never your identity. See Jikan's website for their terms.
  • Google Fonts — The app may download display fonts from Google's font servers. This is a standard request that does not include personal data.

4. Advertising & analytics

Rosome contains no advertising and no analytics SDKs. We do not track your behaviour and there are no third-party trackers in the app.

5. Children's privacy

Rosome is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children. Anime data is requested with a "safe-for-work" filter enabled.

6. Your control over your data

Because all of your data lives on your device, you are always in control. You can remove a title from your library at any time, or uninstall the app to erase everything.

7. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at support@rosoume.com.
