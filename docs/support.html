Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

No. Rosome works with no sign-up and no login. Everything is saved on your device.

How do I add an anime to my list?

On Discover, swipe right to save an anime to "Plan to Watch", or swipe up to love it. You can also open any title and tap Add to Library, then pick a status.

How do I track episode progress?

Open a title from your Library and use the + / − controls, or tap the quick "+1" button on a list row. Reaching the last episode marks it completed.

What is the Aura?

Aura is Rosome's "taste DNA". It reads the genres you watch and turns them into a personal archetype with a unique colour signature, trait scores, and a rarity grade. Find it in the Stats tab.

How do I change the theme or language?

Go to Stats → Settings (gear icon). You can switch between Light, Dark, or System, and choose English, Français, or العربية (with right-to-left layout).

Is my data private?

Yes. Your library and settings stay on your device only. Rosome has no ads, no analytics, and no tracking. See our Privacy Policy.

Where does the anime information come from?

Anime data and cover art are provided by the free Jikan / MyAnimeList API. All artwork belongs to its respective owners.

I found a bug or have a feature idea

We'd love to hear it — email support@rosoume.com with your device model and a short description, and we'll take a look.
